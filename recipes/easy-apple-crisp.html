Easy Apple Crisp

Description

This recipe for apple crisp is adapted from a classic 50-year-old recipe. It works best with fresh, crisp cooking apples.

Ingredients

  • 3 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 125g all-purpose flour (sifted)
  • 200g white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 115g butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C. Lightly grease an 20x20cm (8-inch) square baking dish.
  2. Peel, core, and slice the apples. Arrange them evenly in the prepared baking dish.
  3. In a bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Add the softened butter and cut it in using a pastry blender or two knives until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. Sprinkle the crumb mixture evenly over the apples.
  6. Bake for 40–45 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving. Best enjoyed warm—optionally with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
