Mediterranean Flatbread

Description

These Mediterranean flatbreads are packed with fresh flavor, piled with tomatoes, Kalamata olives, red onion, pepperoncini, and feta cheese. They look a little fancy, but are uncomplicated and perfect for a quick meal or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 flatbreads
  • 60ml olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 cups cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 125g olives, sliced
  • 125g red onion, thinly sliced
  • 80g pepperoncini, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese (or more to taste)
  • Fresh oregano, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the olive oil and chopped garlic. Lightly brush the flatbreads with some of the garlic oil.
  3. Add the halved tomatoes, sliced olives, red onion, and pepperoncini to a bowl. Drizzle with the remaining garlic oil and mix gently.
  4. Evenly distribute the tomato mixture over the flatbreads. Sprinkle with crumbled feta and place them on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the flatbreads turn golden brown. Garnish with chopped oregano and serve warm.
